Real Madrid will play the Asian champion or Guadalajara in the Club World Cup

Real Madrid now know their path in their defence of the Club World Cup. The current champions have two possible opponents in the semi-final on 19 December (5:30pm CET): the Asian champions, who will be confirmed on 10 November, or Mexican side CD Guadalajara. The winners of this match will play Lopetegui’s team for a place in the final. Real Madrid will play all their matches at the Zayed Sports City Stadium in Abu Dhabi.
